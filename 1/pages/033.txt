Countries marked in green are those that have hosted (or are scheduled to host) a Grand Prix in 2025. Those in dark gray had hosted a Formula One Grand Prix in the previous seasons, according to the List of Formula One circuits . Black dots show locations where races are scheduled to take place in 2025, while white dots show locations where races were held in the previous years. Note that for some areas where there were several tracks in close proximity such as Spain, Portugal, Italy, Austria, and USA, the dots may partially overlap. Countries shaded in light gray indicate countries that had never hosted a Formula One Grand Prix before 2025.
